MYCELIUM
A Strategy Game of Fungal World Domination

The Premise

Fungi are the largest, oldest, and most adaptive kingdom of life on Earth. Mycelium asks: what if the most successful kingdom decided humanity was its next evolutionary opportunity?

Each player commands a unique fungal species — real organisms with real capabilities — competing to achieve global dominance through expansion, mutation, and biological warfare.

Turn Structure

  1. Environment Draw — An Environmental Card shifts global conditions.
  2. Growth — Collect resources from every hex you control.
  3. Action Phase — Take 3 actions: expand, draw cards, discard, scavenge.
  4. Reckoning — Human responses escalate as the fungal threat grows.

Resources

B
Biomass
Spend 2 to expand your mycelium into an adjacent hex. Collected per owned hex each round.
S
Spores
Used for long-range dispersal and human infection actions.
MP
Mutation Points
Spend 2 to draw a mutation card. Spend 1 to scavenge from the Spore Drift Pool.

The Hex Map

The world is a 19-region hexagonal grid with 8 terrain types — Alpine, Boreal Forest, Tropical Forest, Temperate Forest, Grassland, Desert, Urban, and Coastal. Each species has a home terrain where they receive a resource bonus. Click adjacent hexes on your turn to expand (costs 2 Biomass).

Mutation Cards

A shared deck of 60 randomized mutation cards sits at the center of the game. Cards span 6 categories:

Substrate Climate Dispersal Vector Defense Special

Draw cards to build your strategy. Each species starts with 3 species-specific mutations already in hand.

Spore Drift Pool

When cards are discarded they enter the Spore Drift Pool — a shared, face-up discard pile. Either player can scavenge from it for 1 MP on their turn. Cards cycle out when the pool exceeds 15.

Victory Paths

  • Population Collapse — Infect and eliminate human populations.
  • Control Network — Dominate human behavior through neurological manipulation.
  • Biomass Empire — Hold territorial dominance for 4 consecutive rounds.
  • Ecological Conquest — Eliminate rival fungal species through mycoparasitism.

2 Players · 90–120 min · Ages 14+

Quick Rules Reference

Actions each turn

  • Expand (2 Biomass) — click a pulsing adjacent hex. Terrain may require a card type.
  • Grow sub-hex (1 Spore) — click an empty dot inside a hex you own.
  • Draw card (2 MP) — one draw per turn from your category deck.
  • Discard — click any card in hand. Goes to the Spore Drift Pool.
  • Scavenge (1 MP) — click the top card in the Drift Pool.

Income (end of each turn)

+1 MP · +1 Biomass per hex · +1 Biomass per fully-grown hex (all 3 dots filled)
